Withdrawal Terms

1. Processing Time

We understand you want to receive your withdrawal as quickly as possible. Our system works continuously to process requests promptly and securely.

Each withdrawal is subject to automated verification and may take between 3 and 120 hours depending on system load and security checks.

If your account passes all internal checks without issues, your withdrawal may be completed in less time.

2. Wallet Address Policy

For security reasons, the same wallet address must not be used across multiple accounts.

3. Cancellation During Processing

You may cancel your withdrawal request at any time during the processing period.

If cancelled before completion, the full amount will be instantly returned to your account balance.

4. Minimum Withdrawal Amount

The minimum withdrawal amount is 20 USDT.
